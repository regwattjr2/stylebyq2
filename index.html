Meet the Interior Decorator

Spaces designed for welcoming.

I’m Q, the lead decorator behind Styled By Q. I believe that your home should be an authentic reflection of the life lived inside it—a warm, intentional place of gathering, rest, and inspiration.

My decorating philosophy draws inspiration from timeless modern farmhouse styling, earth-toned palettes, and layered textures. I focus on making spaces feel organic, beautifully structured, and deeply personal, rather than simply putting together a showroom catalog.

Whether we are working together to completely transform your living space, staging a beautiful listing for a swift sale, or styling the perfect gallery wall, my goal is to guide you with care and detail, delivering a home you will love coming home to.
